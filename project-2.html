PROJECT 02

Dragon

OVERVIEW

Project Overview

The representation of dragons in contemporary animations tends to be homogenized, and it is easy to ignore the richness of history and region. At the same time, traditional patterns lack dynamic visual transformation paths in research, and people often ignore their evolution process.

This work explores the evolution of Chinese dragon patterns with the development of human civilization. In the process of making animations, I mainly used ink and wash to depict, because ink and wash is not only one of the important elements in Chinese painting, but also can make the dragon's form more fluent. At the same time, I combined it with history to mobilize the audience's subjective emotions to help people understand better.

I follow the logic from natural elements (gold, wood, water, fire, earth) to cultural generation to construct the overall direction of the animation.

This project is based on how to express the diversity of Chinese dragons in the form of 2D animation and through colors and lines, and how to combine Chinese dragon patterns with specific cultural elements to help people from different cultures better understand the connotation of Chinese dragons.

This provides a new paradigm for cultural digital protection and plays a role in cultural communication by breaking stereotypes.
