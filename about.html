PROFILE

Creative Background

HI, my name is Yichi Zhang. I’m a 2D animator specializing in storyboarding, with a strong focus on conveying emotion through animation. My work spans character design, story development, and post-production motion graphics, as well as UI animation for interactive software. I strive to create a visual style that is both captivating and impactful, grounded in a sense of harmony. In my storyboarding, I aim to approach narratives from unexpected angles, bringing a fresh visual rhythm and emotional tension to every story I help tell.
